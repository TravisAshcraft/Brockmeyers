About Us

  • Thanks for checking out Brockmeyer’s. Our Pretzels Are Back! A little history, my wife and I founded Baker St. Pretzels, which was a wholesale pretzel bakery delivering authentic pretzels to local breweries in and around the Metro Denver for 7 plus years. Then a heart attack struck at 41 years of age while on my road bike returning from Golden. We decided to close the shop, took some time off, got my health in order and spent the last year building out the Big Top building where we now reside at 6340 W Mississippi Ave, Lakewood. We are serving up made to order espresso, coffee, cocktails with rotating craft brews and wine to accompany our handmade Laugenbrezel, sticky bunns and house made cream cheese spreads. Our menu is simple, we believe in doing a couple items (maybe some variations) but doing it well. We make limited number of our products daily and when we run out that’s it! We hope you stop in and tie one on so to speak.
